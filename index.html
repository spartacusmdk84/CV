My name is David Gillick i am currently in the baby steps of web development and working towards a better me, i have been interested in not only web development for some years but also have been a long time gamer...........very long time! And wanted to transition from my current role as a site foreman (among many other roles i have had over the years!) to more a web development/indie games developer role, i am 35 years old and still feel as though im not going to let time pass me by, and to grab my life by its reigns and get amongst the code and intricacies of what i need to learn to be able to share my love with the world.
